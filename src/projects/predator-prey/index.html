Predator-Prey

September 15, 2020

In this project, I created a cellular automaton width adjustable parameters to model a predator and prey dynamic. The simulation illustrates population fluctuation and demonstrates the codependent nature of a predator-prey relationship.

The parameters of the simulation can be modified under the settings tab.
